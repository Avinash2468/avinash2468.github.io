Hey! I’m a junior year student at International Institute of Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT- Hyderabad) studying Electronics and Communications Engineering.

Working on active research problems has been a passion of mine ever since I joined IIIT-H. I enjoy working on problems which lie at the intersection of Computer Vision and Robotics.

I’m currently an honours research student in the Robotics Research Center, IIIT-H advised by Dr. Madhava Krishna. I’m currently working on generating datasets to solve problems in free space estimation and multi-robot perception and planning in warehouse settings.

Over the summer of 2020, I worked as a computer vision intern at Bot AI ML. I was involved in improving their vehicle detection and localisation system.

I am also the technical director of the electronics and robotics club at IIIT-Hyderabad. In the club, we hold regular sessions to introduce newcomers into the field of robotics.
