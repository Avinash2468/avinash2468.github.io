Hey! I’m a junior year student at [International Institute of Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT- Hyderabad)][IIIT] studying Electronics and Communications Engineering.

Working on research problems has been a passion of mine ever since I joined IIIT-H. Consequently,I joined [Robotics Research Center, IIIT-H][RRC] as an honors research student advised by [Dr. Madhava Krishna][Madhav sir]. I'm deeply passionate about the field of Computer Vision and Robotics with a particular interest in perception tasks for robots. As part of my research, I’m currently working on generating datasets to solve problems in free space estimation for multi-robot systems in warehouse settings.

Over the summer of 2020, I worked as a computer vision intern at [Bot AI ML][Bot]. I was involved in improving their vehicle detection and localisation system. It was my first experience working with a team of professionals in the field of Computer Vision and I learnt a lot such as using transfer learning with existing models and fine tuning them for our custom use case. I also got to work with large data sets which made me appreciate the work that goes behind generating data sets.

I am also the technical director of the [Electronics and Robotics club][Club] at IIIT-Hyderabad. I work with a team of 7 people to carry out sessions to introduce newcomers to the fields of robotics and vision. We also mentor and guide students who are working on projects associated with the club.
